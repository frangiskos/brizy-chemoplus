Company Profile

Chemoplus Ltd is active in the import of sealants, adhesives, and lubricants for the professional user. Our range includes acrylic sealants, silicones, hybrid polymers, polyurethane sealants, polyurethane foams as well as accessories . Our goal is to offer high and consistent quality products for the professional user.
